## Common Issues

### Issue: Colors not printing
**Fix:** Enable "Background graphics" or "Print backgrounds"

### Issue: Content cut off
**Fix:** Select A6 paper size, not A4 or Letter

### Issue: Text too small
**Fix:** Increase scale to 110% or 120%

### Issue: Print button doesn't work
**Fix:** Refresh page (Ctrl+R) and try again
