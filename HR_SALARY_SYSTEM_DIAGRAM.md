## Quick Reference

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                           QUICK REFERENCE                                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

STAFF TYPES:
  🎓 TEACHER        - Teaching staff
  🔧 SUPPORTIVE     - Support staff (janitors, guards, etc.)
  💼 ADMINISTRATIVE - Admin staff (clerks, managers, etc.)

DEDUCTION TYPES:
  📊 TAX            - Income tax (default 15%)
  🏦 PENSION        - Pension fund (default 7%)
  ⚙️  SERVICE       - Service charge (default 2%)
  💳 CREDIT         - Loan/credit deduction (default 0%)

ALLOWANCE TYPES:
  🏠 HOUSING        - Housing allowance (default $500)
  🚗 TRANSPORT      - Transport allowance (default $200)
  🏥 MEDICAL        - Medical allowance (default $150)
  🍽️  FOOD          - Food allowance (default $100)

RETENTION BENEFITS:
  🎓 TUITION WAIVER - For staff children (default $1000)
  🏆 MERIT PAY      - Performance bonus (default 10%)

CALCULATION TYPES:
  💵 FIXED          - Fixed dollar amount
  📈 PERCENTAGE     - Percentage of base salary

FORMULA:
  Net Salary = Base + Allowances + Retention - Deductions
```
